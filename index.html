Example of my work: Xalians.com

This is a pet project I started a while back in the 2021 crypto craze. I dove into the idea of using the Ethereum blockchain to host "Pokemon-like" digital creatures (backed by non-fungible token technology) that could be uniquely minted and owned by individuals. These "Xalian" creatures would be collectable, tradeable, and eventually could be used in an online gaming ecosystem built around the creatures. I still love the idea, but at this time the Ethereum chain has not yet scaled to allow for low enough fees for mass adoption of an idea like this. I built the entire site and created all art myself, while working with my amazing brother-in-law writer to help create the lore and story behind the creatures.
