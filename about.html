About Nick Jordan

A Simple Bio

From the time he was a small child, Nick has always taken a high interest in technology. Coming out of high school, Nick earned a scholarship to play football for The University of Texas, and came to Austin with the mindset that he wanted to be a Computer Engineer. After his first few classes, Nick realized his shifting interest towards software. After overseeing some school work of a friend majoring in Computer Science, Nick quickly developed an interest in this field of study, and decided to internally transfer to The College of Natural Science and begin pursung his degree in Computer Science. It wasn't long after his first programming class that he realized his passion for Computer Science.
